Professional Paint Correction

Our paint correction service is the ultimate solution for restoring your vehicle's paintwork to its original glory. Using advanced techniques and premium products, we carefully remove imperfections that regular washing can't fix, revealing a deep, glossy finish that enhances your car's appearance and protects its value.

Our Process:

  • Inspection & Analysis: Thorough examination of your vehicle's paint condition under different lighting conditions.
  • Decontamination: Removal of surface contaminants, tar, and industrial fallout.
  • Paint Measurement: Using advanced tools to measure paint thickness and determine the appropriate correction level.
  • Multi-Stage Correction: Precise machine polishing to remove swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation.
  • Final Polish: Refining the finish to achieve maximum gloss and clarity.
  • Protection: Application of premium sealant or ceramic coating to protect the corrected finish.

Correction Levels:

Light Correction

Ideal for well-maintained vehicles with minor imperfections.

  • Removes light swirl marks
  • Enhances gloss and clarity
  • 1-2 stage correction
  • Perfect for newer vehicles

Moderate Correction

For vehicles with moderate swirls and light scratches.

  • Removes moderate swirls and light scratches
  • 2-3 stage correction
  • Significant improvement in appearance
  • Ideal for daily drivers

Full Correction

For heavily swirled or neglected paintwork.

  • Removes severe swirls and scratches
  • 3+ stage correction
  • Dramatic transformation
  • Showroom-quality results
