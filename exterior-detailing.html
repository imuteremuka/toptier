Professional Exterior Detailing

Our comprehensive exterior detailing service is designed to bring back your vehicle's original shine and provide long-lasting protection against the elements. Using premium products and professional techniques, we'll make your car look like it just rolled off the showroom floor.

Service Includes:

  • Hand Wash & Dry: Gentle hand wash with premium car shampoo followed by a thorough drying with microfiber towels to prevent water spots.
  • Clay Bar Treatment: Removes embedded contaminants from your paint that regular washing can't eliminate.
  • Paint Decontamination: Removes industrial fallout, tree sap, and other stubborn contaminants.
  • Wheel & Tire Detailing: Deep cleaning of wheels, wheel wells, and tires, followed by tire dressing.
  • Glass & Mirror Cleaning: Streak-free cleaning of all exterior glass surfaces for maximum visibility.
  • Exterior Polish & Wax: Professional polishing to remove light scratches and swirl marks, followed by high-quality wax application for deep shine and protection.

Benefits:

Paint Protection

Shields your vehicle's paint from UV rays, acid rain, and environmental contaminants.

UV Protection

Prevents paint fading and oxidation caused by sun exposure.

Hydrophobic Effect

Water beads up and rolls off, making future cleaning easier.

Resale Value

Maintains your vehicle's value by protecting its exterior finish.
