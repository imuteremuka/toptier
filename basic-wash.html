Professional Basic Wash Service

Our Basic Wash is the perfect solution for maintaining your vehicle's appearance between more detailed services. This quick yet thorough cleaning will leave your car looking fresh and well-maintained, removing dirt, dust, and grime from both the exterior and interior.

Service Includes:

  • Exterior Hand Wash: Gentle hand washing using premium car shampoo to safely remove dirt and grime.
  • Wheel & Tire Cleaning: Thorough cleaning of wheels, wheel wells, and tires to remove brake dust and road grime.
  • Windows & Mirrors: Streak-free cleaning of all exterior glass surfaces for maximum visibility.
  • Door Jambs: Cleaning of door jambs and sills to remove built-up dirt and grime.
  • Interior Vacuuming: Complete vacuuming of carpets, seats, and cargo area.
  • Interior Wipe Down: Dusting and cleaning of dashboard, console, and other interior surfaces.
