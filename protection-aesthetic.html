Premium Protection & Aesthetic Services

Our premium protection and aesthetic services go beyond basic cleaning to preserve and enhance your vehicle's appearance. Using only the highest quality products and techniques, we'll help protect your investment and keep your car looking its best for years to come.

Ceramic Coating

Our professional ceramic coating provides long-lasting protection and an incredible shine that lasts for years. This nanotechnology forms a semi-permanent bond with your vehicle's paint, creating an ultra-durable, hydrophobic layer that resists dirt, chemicals, and UV rays.

Benefits:

  • 2-5 years of protection (depending on package)
  • Extreme water beading and sheeting
  • UV protection to prevent fading
  • Chemical resistance against road salts and contaminants
  • Enhanced gloss and depth of color

Wax & Sealant Application

Our premium wax and sealant applications provide excellent protection and shine for your vehicle's paintwork. Choose between traditional carnauba wax for a deep, warm glow or synthetic sealants for longer-lasting protection.

Synthetic Sealant

  • Superior durability

Paintless Dent Removal (PDR)

Our professional paintless dent removal service can repair minor dents, dings, and creases without the need for repainting, preserving your vehicle's original factory finish.

Benefits of PDR:

  • No need for repainting
  • Maintains original factory finish
  • Quick turnaround time
  • Environmentally friendly
  • Cost-effective solution

* PDR is suitable for dents where the paint is still intact. Our technicians will assess if your dent is a good candidate for PDR.
