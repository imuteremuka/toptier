Professional Ceramic Coating

Our premium ceramic coating service provides long-lasting protection for your vehicle's paint, keeping it looking showroom-new for years to come. This advanced liquid polymer bonds with your vehicle's factory paint, creating a layer of protection that resists environmental contaminants, UV rays, and minor scratches.

Service Includes:

  • Paint Decontamination: Complete removal of surface contaminants, tar, and industrial fallout.
  • Paint Correction: Multi-stage polishing to remove swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation.
  • Surface Preparation: Thorough cleaning and preparation to ensure optimal bonding.
  • Ceramic Coating Application: Professional application of high-quality ceramic coating.
  • Curing Time: Proper curing under controlled conditions for maximum durability.
  • Final Inspection: Quality check to ensure perfect application.

Benefits:

Long-Lasting Protection

2+ years of durable protection against environmental elements.

Hydrophobic Effect

Water beads and rolls off the surface, making cleaning easier.

UV Protection

Prevents paint fading and oxidation caused by sun exposure.

Value Retention

Maintains your vehicle's appearance and resale value.
